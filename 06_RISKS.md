# Risks And Guardrails

## Product Guardrails
- Source-first: every important field should map back to a source document
- Draft-only on critical outputs
- Manual review gates before final delivery
- Rule-based logic for deadlines, required fields and document completeness
- AI only for extraction, drafting and assistance

## Founder Risks
- No formal environmental education
- Risk of overpromising autonomous correctness
- Risk of being pulled into engineering-heavy work
- Risk of endless custom service without product discipline

## Red Flags
- No source docs at all
- Buyer is unclear
- Client wants "everything at once"
- Complex hazardous / disputed cases as first scope
- Multi-site chaos before a repeatable process exists
- Wants legal/expert guarantee without review

## Scope Limits For Early Clients
- 1 legal entity
- 1 site by default
- up to 8 typical waste streams
- no water/emissions heavy scope
- no complex engineering

## Sales Guardrails
- Do not say "we replace your ecologist"
- Do not say "fully automatic compliance"
- Do not promise final legal/environmental correctness without review
- Sell drafts, completeness, order, reminders and managed support
- Use source-doc language in proposals and calls

## Track-Specific Warnings
- Waste:
  - avoid becoming cheap document shop
- ROP:
  - avoid mini-ERP and over-integration
- Auto:
  - avoid broad "ecology for auto" scope
- Partners:
  - avoid building network before intros
- Expansion:
  - avoid engineering-heavy module activation
