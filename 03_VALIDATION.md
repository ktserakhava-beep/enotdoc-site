# Validation Summary

## Core Hypothesis
- SMB in Belarus without in-house ecologist will pay for structured waste-compliance setup + monthly support.

## Strong Market Signals Already Found
- Commercial services exist for:
  - 1-отходы
  - ПОД-9/10
  - инструкции по отходам
- Existing prices visible from 250 BYN to 450 BYN for separate blocks
- Tender evidence exists for waste, emissions, water and related eco-work
- Hiring signals exist for ecologists and environmental engineers

## Why Waste First
- Strong payment visibility
- Clear buyer and urgent pain
- Lower engineering risk
- Better solo launchability
- Easier service-to-product transition

## Why ROP Second
- Strong rule-based and data-heavy workflow
- Quarterly recurring cycle
- Better software moat potential
- Worse visibility and more mixed ownership of process

## Why Auto Third
- Strong vertical packaging
- Good for multi-site rollouts
- Better after waste templates exist

## Stage 3 Success Criteria
- 1–3 paid setups or paid diagnostics
- 5–7 strong buying signals
- Repeatable ICP pattern
- 12–20 discovery / sales calls in 30 days

## Working Validation Thresholds
- Waste:
  - 1 paid setup is enough to continue if ICP pattern is clean
  - 2+ paid setups gives strong confirmation
- ROP:
  - 3–5 discovery conversations
  - 1 proposal or paid audit/setup indicates second-track viability
- Auto:
  - 1 warm lead plus clear vertical fit is enough to keep alive
  - 1 paid pilot is enough to justify packaging work
- Partners:
  - 3+ warm intros and 1 paid start from channel is enough to keep investing
- Expansion:
  - activate only on repeating inbound asks from live client base
