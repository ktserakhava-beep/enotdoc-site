# Decisions Log

## 2026-04-05
- Decision: Беларусь — основной рынок исследования.
- Reason: нужен практический B2B/B2B2G wedge с локальной нормативкой и понятной болью.

## 2026-04-06
- Decision: Первый wedge — Waste compliance, а не РОП, авто, вода или выбросы.
- Reason: проще продать, ниже кастомщина, понятнее buyer, быстрее первый revenue.

## 2026-04-06
- Decision: РОП держать как second track, а не как первый GTM.
- Reason: сильный software-fit, но mixed buyer и хуже публично видимый рынок услуг.

## 2026-04-06
- Decision: Авто не брать как отдельный first market.
- Reason: лучше работает как vertical package поверх waste-core.

## 2026-04-06
- Decision: Партнеров не строить как большую программу на старте.
- Reason: сначала нужен partner pre-check через warm intros / referral.

## 2026-04-06
- Decision: Water / Emissions / Ecopassport — later expansion modules.
- Reason: для старта слишком высок риск инженерки, лабораторий и тяжелой проектности.

## 2026-04-06
- Decision: Истина проекта живет в локальных файлах на ПК, а не в чатах.
- Reason: это снижает потерю контекста и позволяет работать в 1–3 чатах.

## 2026-04-06
- Decision: First outreach wave for Waste uses one default ICP only: small/mid production or packaging company in Belarus with 1 legal entity, 1 site, 15-120 employees, no in-house ecologist.
- Reason: This matches current Waste guardrails and keeps first sales inside the safe scope without drifting into multi-site, hazardous-heavy, water, emissions, or other expanded work.

## 2026-04-06
- Decision: First digital MVP is an intake-first web MVP for the Waste offer, not a big site, not a full SaaS, and not a GPT-first product.
- Reason: The current monetization path is `24h fit-check -> scope -> proposal -> setup + monthly support`, so the first digital layer should capture and qualify inbound cases while keeping human review and source-doc guardrails.

## 2026-04-06
- Decision: External face of the first MVP is a single `landing + intake page` focused only on Waste Compliance Setup + Monthly Support.
- Reason: This is the smallest professional-looking surface that both explains the offer and moves prospects into the fit-check flow without spreading into multi-track navigation or content-heavy marketing work.

## 2026-04-06
- Decision: Internal engine of the first MVP is a lightweight operator workflow in Excel/table format, with manual review, qualification, fit-check summary, and proposal prep.
- Reason: It matches current operating reality, protects quality through human-in-the-loop review, and avoids premature engineering before real inbound patterns are visible.

## 2026-04-06
- Decision: Build order for the first product layer is `landing + intake page -> lightweight internal dashboard/workspace -> structured wizard -> GPT summary layer`.
- Reason: This sequence supports early sales first, then operator efficiency, and only later adds structured self-serve input or AI summarization once enough real cases exist.
