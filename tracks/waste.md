# Track 01 - Waste

## Role
- Core revenue wedge

## Default ICP
- Small/mid production or packaging company in Belarus
- 1 site
- 15–120 employees
- No in-house ecologist

## Secondary ICP
- Wholesale / warehouse / distribution / e-commerce fulfillment with 1 warehouse

## Qualification
- Not a micro-organization
- 1 legal entity, 1 site
- 3–10 waste streams
- At least some source docs exist
- Buyer is clear
- Needs result in 7–30 days
- Wants done-for-you outcome, not just a template

## Disqualification
- 3+ sites with different waste flows
- Complex hazardous / disputed waste at core of case
- Wants only a blank template
- No acts / contracts / old docs and no willingness to gather them
- Wants water/air/ecopassport in the same first scope

## Default Offer
- Waste Compliance Setup + Monthly Support
- Packaging: ПОД + 1-отходы + инструкция + акты + дедлайны

## Default Pricing Test
- 1 490 BYN setup + 390 BYN/month
- Backup: 690 BYN paid audit

## CTA
- Send current instruction / latest 1-отходы / 2–3 acts for 24-hour fit-check

## What To Do Now
- Build ICP list
- Run outreach
- Close paid setup
- Standardize delivery
- Use fit-check CTA on live documents
- Capture objections and update qualification rules

## What Not To Do
- No complex engineering
- No multi-site rollout at the start
- No custom ERP integration

## Offer Logic
- Sell order, continuity and readiness for checks
- Do not sell a single document in isolation
- Best first promise: restore working waste-compliance base in 7–14 days and hold monthly continuity

## First 3 Clients Path
1. Warm intro through founder / accountant / labor-safety contact
2. Targeted outbound to SMB with simple waste profile
3. Partner intro through accounting outsourcing or waste contractor

## Core Risks
- Getting stuck as low-margin manual service
- Over-customizing to win early clients
- Accepting cases with hidden air/water/hazardous complexity

## Anti-Goals For First 30 Days
- Do not take multi-site by default
- Do not reduce price below healthy floor for messy cases
- Do not promise full autonomous correctness
- Do not expand scope inside first proposal
