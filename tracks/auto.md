# Track 03 - Auto

## Role
- Vertical package on top of waste-core

## Default ICP
- Network of STOs or dealer service group
- 3–8 sites
- Repeating oils/tires/acts/contractors problem

## Secondary ICP
- Centralized fleet with 2+ bases and own service area / maintenance zone

## Qualification
- 3+ sites or 2+ bases
- Regular oils / filters / tires waste
- Central owner of process exists
- Real pain in acts, contractors, site coordination or calendar
- Willing to start with 1–2 pilot sites

## Disqualification
- Single small STO with no rollout potential
- Start from автомойки or water-heavy pain
- No own oils/tires flow
- Wants "all ecology for car business" from day one
- No central coordinator

## Default Offer
- Auto Waste Ops Pack
- Oils + tires + acts + contractors + calendar by sites

## Default CTA
- 45-minute review of 2 sites and current contractor/acts map

## Default Pricing Logic
- Paid starter setup for 1–2 sites + monthly support

## What To Do Now
- Keep vertical positioning ready
- Validate only through warm leads or after first waste cases
- Reuse waste-core templates and operating model
- Look for multi-site rollout logic, not one-off local cleanup

## What Not To Do
- Do not treat auto as broad standalone market
- Do not start with автомойки
- Do not mix in water/emissions too early

## Activation Trigger
- At least 1–2 successful waste cases
- Warm intro into auto network / fleet / dealer group
- Clear signal that multi-site site-order pain is real

## Success Signal
- 2+ auto leads understand offer without extra education
- 1 paid pilot on 1–2 sites
- Rollout logic becomes reusable

## Anti-Goals
- No full environmental servicing for auto business
- No jump into washing/stormwater/air complexity
- No bespoke fleet management product drift
