# Track 04 - Partners

## Role
- Channel layer

## Default Partner Type
- Accounting outsourcing firms with SMB portfolio

## Secondary Partner Type
- Waste contractors / recyclers / disposal operators

## Later Partner Types
- Eco-consultants
- VED / ERP / 1C partners

## Default Ask
- 1–2 warm intros to clients with clear waste document pain

## Qualification
- Real SMB client base in Belarus
- Repeating contact with waste/reporting/import/document pain
- Ready to do warm intros, not just "look at presentation"
- Understands value after 1 short conversation
- No direct 1:1 conflict with your fixed-scope waste offer

## Disqualification
- Wants exclusivity or white-label before first deal
- Cannot name relevant client type
- Needs long enablement before any intro
- Avoids discussion of referral scope or incentive
- Has no trusted-advisor position with client

## What To Do Now
- Partner pre-check only
- Validate intro flow
- Start with accountants, then waste contractors
- Keep eco-consultants selective and later

## What Not To Do
- No big partner program
- No white-label too early
- No exclusivity

## Default Partner Motion
- Warm intro / referral first
- Co-delivery only if deal is simple and channel clearly adds trust
- Revenue share only when there is actual lead flow

## Success Signal
- 3+ warm intros in 30 days
- 2+ relevant client conversations
- 1 paid start from partner channel

## Anti-Goals
- Do not build fancy partner deck/program before first intros
- Do not chase ERP/1C channel too early
- Do not let partners push you into scope expansion
